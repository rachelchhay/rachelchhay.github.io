Welcome to Rachel's Restaurant Rush!

The goal of the game is to complete the customer orders by clicking on the menu items. You have to click on the items in the same order as they appear in the Customer Order speech bubble. Whatever you click on will show up in your Orders Ready speech bubble. Once your order is complete, click on the "Order Up" button at the bottom. If your order is correct, you will gain 1 coin and 2 XP. If your order is incorrect, you will lose 1 coin and 2 XP. Click the "Next Customer" button when you're ready for the next order.

In each round, you have a set number of coins you need to earn to move on. There are 3 rounds total. For round 1, you need to earn 4 coins. If you are down to 0 coins and 0 XP, you lose the game. Complete all 3 rounds, and you win the game.

Good luck and have fun!
